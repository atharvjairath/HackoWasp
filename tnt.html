About Us

We provide great exposure to ACTING in the form of drama

Thapar Nautanki Team is a street play group from Thapar Institute of Engg & Tech, Patiala. It is a team of students who come together & take a stand on social issues prevailing in the society. We try to present all the effective points via 'nukkad natak'.

TNT (Thapar Nautanki Team) is a team of students from Thapar University .,Being a part of the youth of India we believe that we should work towards building our country by supporting the good and attempting to bring positive changes in the society .

Our team tries to shape public opinion by exposing frantic and rampant social malpractices for the betterment of society through the traditional art of Nukkad Natak which not only allows us to reach a large population but also helps in keeping this art form alive.
