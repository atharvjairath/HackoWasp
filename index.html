Societies

Various Societies Of Thapar University

Owasp

The OWASP student chapter provides an opportunity to the students to work and develop skills in the field of web and mobile applications, and computer information security

Thapar Nautanki Team

Thapar Nautanki Team is a street play group from Thapar Institute of Engg & Tech, Patiala. It is a team of students who come together & take a stand on social issues prevailing in the society. We try to present all the effective points via 'nukkad natak'.

Adventure-Club

Adventure Club at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology is the forum for adventure enthusiasts, trekkers, climbers and armchair mountaineers in the student community. It works to develop the spirit of adventure in Thaparians.
